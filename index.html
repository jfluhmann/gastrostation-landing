The reality

Compliance shouldn't be a
second full-time job.

City health permits. State DSHS licenses. Fire marshal inspections. Vehicle registration. Commissary agreements. Food handler cards. Liability insurance. All on different schedules, from different agencies, with different consequences for lapsing.

01

No roadmap exists

New operators discover requirements piecemeal — often after getting cited or fined. There's no single guide that walks you through everything.

02

Renewals slip through

Different permits expire at different times. Most operators are running a 3-ring binder, a Google Calendar reminder, and a folder of scanned PDFs. When something lapses, they find out from an inspector.

03

Nothing is built for this

Generic document tools don't know what a mobile food unit permit is. Generic reminder apps don't understand renewal cycles. No food truck compliance software currently exists. Gastro Station is the first.

Introducing

Gastro Station Comply

The compliance management platform built specifically for mobile food businesses. Know before you roll.

Onboarding Wizard

A step-by-step checklist that walks you through every permit, license, and certificate required to legally operate — in the right order, with links to the right agencies and forms. The guide every new operator wishes they had on day one.

  • Categorized by agency (city, state, federal)
  • Direct links to applications and forms
  • Cost estimates and processing timelines
  • Progress tracker to "fully legal" status

Document Vault

Every permit, license, and certificate in one secure place. Organized by category with expiration tracking built in. No more digging through filing cabinets or camera rolls.

Renewal Alerts

Email reminders at 60, 30, and 7 days before anything expires. Dashboard indicators show what's current, expiring soon, or already lapsed. Nothing falls through the cracks.

Texas-Specific Intelligence

Pre-loaded with Texas DSHS mobile food unit requirements and city-specific permit details. Direct links to renewal forms and agency contacts baked right in. Expanding to new states as we grow.
